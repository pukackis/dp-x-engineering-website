Industrial Machine Part (2023)

Design and optimization of a critical component for heavy industrial machinery, focusing on durability and performance under extreme conditions.

Project Overview

This project involved the detailed mechanical design of a custom part for a large industrial machine. The primary challenge was to create a component that could withstand high stresses, vibrations, and corrosive environments, ensuring long-term reliability and minimal maintenance.

We conducted extensive material selection analysis and performed advanced finite element analysis (FEA) to simulate various load cases and optimize the geometry for maximum strength-to-weight ratio. Special attention was paid to manufacturing tolerances for seamless integration into existing assemblies.

Key Challenges & Solutions

  • Extreme Conditions: Selected high-strength, corrosion-resistant alloys and applied specialized coatings.
  • Fatigue Life: Optimized geometry to minimize stress concentrations and improve fatigue resistance, validated through simulation.
  • Manufacturing Precision: Designed with tight tolerances and specified manufacturing processes (e.g., CNC machining) to ensure exact fit and function.
