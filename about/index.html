About Us

DP-X Engineering was founded by passionate mechanical engineers with hands-on industry experience in both Poland and Germany. We combine solid engineering principles with practical understanding of manufacturing, delivering designs that work in the real world.

Whether it’s a single component or a complete assembly – we approach each task with precision, creativity, and responsibility.

Our Founders

  • Szymon Pukacki – Mechanical Engineer, Poland
  • Michał Dydecki – Mechanical Engineer, Germany

Our Philosophy

At DP-X Engineering, we believe that successful design is a blend of technical expertise, creative problem-solving, and a deep understanding of manufacturing processes. Our approach is rooted in a commitment to delivering not just designs, but solutions that are practical, efficient, and ready for real-world application.

We foster a collaborative environment, working closely with our clients from concept to completion. This ensures that every project aligns perfectly with their vision and business objectives. Our dedication to continuous learning and adoption of the latest technologies keeps us at the forefront of mechanical design.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced Team: Our engineers bring years of industry experience from diverse sectors.
  • Innovative Solutions: We thrive on complex challenges, delivering creative and effective designs.
  • Manufacturing Focus: Designs are optimized for production, reducing costs and lead times.
  • Client-Centric Approach: Your success is our priority, and we work tirelessly to achieve it.
