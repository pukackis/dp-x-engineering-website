Our Comprehensive Services

At DP-X Engineering, we offer a full spectrum of mechanical design services, from initial concept to production-ready documentation. Our expertise ensures that your projects are not only innovative but also practical and manufacturable.

3D CAD Modeling & Assemblies

We create precise and detailed 3D models of individual components and complex assemblies using industry-leading software. Our models are built for accuracy, functionality, and ease of modification, serving as the foundation for all subsequent design and manufacturing processes.

Technical Drawings & Manufacturing Documentation

Our team produces comprehensive technical drawings and manufacturing documentation that adhere to international standards (ISO, ASME). This includes detailed part drawings, assembly drawings, bills of materials (BOMs), and process sheets, ensuring clear communication with manufacturers.

Reverse Engineering

When original design data is unavailable, we can reverse engineer existing physical parts or assemblies. Using 3D scanning and advanced CAD techniques, we accurately recreate digital models and drawings, enabling reproduction, modification, or analysis of legacy components.

Design Optimization & Consulting

We help you enhance existing designs for improved performance, reduced costs, and enhanced manufacturability. Our consulting services leverage advanced simulation tools (FEA, CFD) and our deep industry knowledge to identify areas for improvement and implement effective solutions.

Product Development

From initial concept to final production, we guide you through the entire product development lifecycle. Our holistic approach covers ideation, conceptual design, detailed engineering, prototyping support, and preparation for mass production, ensuring a seamless transition from idea to market.

We use professional software (Siemens NX, SolidWorks, Inventor) and ensure full support from concept to production.
